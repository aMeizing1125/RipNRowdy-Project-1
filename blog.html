OMB Always Excellent

In my opinion, the first brewery to establish Charlotte as a major producer of quality Craft Beer. OMB has the unique ability to never make a beer I wouldn't order again. Everything on the menu is very well balanced beer and food. Enjoying the atmosphere while sitting under a shaded tree eating some German potato salad with a delicious Hornest's Nest beer to wash it down. Sounds like the most perfect day. Adding this location to your personal bar crawl will always be a crowd pleaser. It is a must on every beer drinkers checklist.

Not Your Ordinary Beer

Having Lenny Boy born right here in Charlotte, has spoiled all us with amazing Kombucha and Beer. They have an amazing selection of non-alocholic if that is your fancy beverages. My personal favorite is the Laughing Yoga. With hints of Jasime and just bringing life to your summer and embracing your inner outdoor yogi. Lenny Boy also supports other local breweries by rotating the other local craft beer. Why compet when we can be friends?!

Carolina Cider - Red Clay Ciderworks

Everyone has a friend who can only drink Cider for it wonderful attribute, Gluten-Free. Red Clay is so good you'll drink it anyway. Playing to all pallets, Clay Works offers a variety of tart crips flavor bouqets to sweet lusciousness leaving you wanting more. This isn't your average Cider. Made from real North Carolina apples.

Noda Company Store & Asian Flush

First, yes not a brewery, but they sell the craft brews, and... Asian Flush? What is that. It is a common allergy predominatently Asian people have. Basically, I am allergic to alcohol, so I have an accellerated reaction to EVERYONE ELSE. I absorb it faster, and sober faster. I will turn red. It always starts on the cheeks and goes down. Not to worry, this also means I sober up super quick. It only took a beer anyway.
Now on to authentic deliciousness that is the Dumpling Lady. Everyone needs to make a stop at the Noda Company Store when the Dumpling Lady is there. She is a weekly regular Food Truck and provides some of the most true Chinese food. Sorry guys, I have nevereaten a Crab Ragoon or Sweet & Sour Chicken in Taiwan, China, or Japan. And you are in the heart of Noda which makes your personal Bar Crawl an ideal place to start.

Last updated Oct. 2018
Legion adds Excellence in SouthPark

Legion Brewery has an quality reputation among Charlotte Beer Nuts. But their new location in SouthPark has exceeded my high expectations. One of the owner is from Shelby, NC. That was my place to go when I was in my smalltown. Don't let smalltown fool you, this place is True Excellence. This place has amazing flavors. The menu is well put together and with a full bar, none of your guests will feel pressure to drink beer. My go to is the Sparkle Party, they have 4 flavors. Enjoy getting to know each one. You won't be disappointed.
